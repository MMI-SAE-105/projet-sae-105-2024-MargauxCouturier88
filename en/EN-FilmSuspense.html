Thriller movies Gallery

Welcome to our selection of thrillers featuring trains, old or futuristic!

The trains, with their intriguing atmosphere, provide the perfect setting for captivating stories. Whether you are seduced by the elegance of steam locomotives or the advanced technologies of modern trains, you will find thrillers that will keep you in suspense.

Get ready to embark on thrilling adventures, where the thrill of travel mingles with surprising revelations!

The Passenger

Director : Jaume Collet-Serra

Year of release : 2018

The Passenger is a thrilling thriller starring Laura, an ordinary man travelling by train. When he meets a mysterious woman who offers him a challenge to identify a hidden passenger, he quickly discovers that he is involved in a criminal plot.

What starts as a harmless game becomes a race against the clock when his life and that of other passengers is in danger. With unexpected twists and turns, the film explores themes of morality and survival in extreme situations.

Le train sifflera trois fois

Director : Fred Zinnemann

Year of release : 1952

This film is a classic western released in 1952. The story takes place in a small town in the American West, where sheriff Will Kane is preparing to leave his post and start a new life with his wife, Amy. However, he learns that a dangerous criminal, Frank Miller, whom he has arrested in the past, is back in town to take revenge.

As the time of Miller’s train approach, Kane must face his fear and sense of duty. Despite the lack of support from the locals, he stands ready to defend the city.

Source code

Director : Duncan Jones

Year of release : 2011

This film follows Captain Colter Stevens, a military helicopter pilot. After being fatally wounded in a mission, he wakes up in an unknown body on a train bound for Chicago, just before an explosion that will kill all the passengers.

Colter discovers that he is involved in a government program called “Source Code”, which allows him to relive the last eight minutes before the disaster. His mission is to find the person responsible for the attack and prevent further attacks. At each iteration, he questions the passengers and tries to understand the events leading up to the explosion.
