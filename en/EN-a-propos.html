About

My name is Margaux Couturier, a first-year student in the MMI program at the IUT in Montbéliard.

Through this project, I wanted to explore the vision of a train in the distant future.

I imagine it levitating through magnetic energy, sporting a luxurious design while promoting sustainable development. This futuristic train would also be much faster, reducing the time required for long journeys as we know it today.
