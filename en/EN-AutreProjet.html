Other project

On this page, you will discover a selection of fascinating projects that explore how various everyday objects could evolve in the future. These creative visions imagine technological, aesthetic and functional transformations, allowing us to glimpse how these familiar objects could adapt to the needs of future generations. Whether it’s transportation, communication tools or household items, each project offers a unique reflection on the impact of innovation and imagination on our way of life.
