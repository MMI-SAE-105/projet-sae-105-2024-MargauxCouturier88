Action Film Gallery

Welcome to our page dedicated to action films in which trains play a central role, whether old or futuristic.

Whether you're a fan of steam locomotives steeped in history or futuristic magnetic levitation trains, our selection offers you a cinematic journey through thrilling tales that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Get ready to climb aboard for an unforgettable experience full of plot twists and breathtaking action!

Snowpiercer : Le Transperceneige

Director : Bong Joon-ho

Year of release : 2013

The film is set in an icy world where humanity's last refuge is a perpetually moving train, the Snowpiercer. Passengers are divided into social classes, the rich living in the comfort of the front carriages, while the poor suffer precarious conditions at the back.

Curtis leads a revolt against this oppression, leading to violent confrontations and hard-hitting revelations. With striking aesthetics and incisive storytelling, Snowpiercer tackle’s themes of class warfare and survival, while delivering gripping action at every turn of the journey.

Unstoppable

Director : Tony Scott

Year of release : 2010

The film is an action thriller based on true events that follows the story of an out-of-control goods train. After a train full of dangerous goods is accidentally released, an experienced engineer, Frank Barnes, and a young driver, Will Colson, race against time to try and stop the train before it causes a catastrophe.

As the train approaches a populated town, the tension mounts, and the two men must overcome various obstacles to avert an impending tragedy. With energetic direction and captivating performances, “Unstoppable” explores themes of courage, camaraderie and the importance of responsibility in the face of the unexpected.

Dernier train pour Busan

Director : Sang-Ho Yeon

Year of release : 2016

The film follows a group of passengers on board a high-speed train bound for Busan. As they set off, a zombie epidemic breaks out, threatening the lives of everyone on board.

Among the passengers, a father, Seok-woo, and his daughter, Soo-an, must cope with the growing panic and terror as they try to survive. As the train moves on, tensions between the passengers rise, and difficult choices must be made to escape the threat. With a tense atmosphere and intense action scenes.
